**Tagline:** Turn any webpage into a shared live experience with flowing audience comments.

**Description:** GemMurmur is a Chrome extension that brings audience reactions directly onto the webpage. In normal mode, comments flow from right to left across the screen. In Buzz mode, colourful reactions fill the entire page. Local Gemma and multilingual support keep the experience private, responsive, and welcoming across languages.
